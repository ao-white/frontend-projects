About Us

Welcome to the Grub Stranding website! We serve a variety of food that will leave you satiated and wanting to come back for more. We serve American classics such as hamburgers and hot dogs, all made with the freshest ingredients to deliver the best tasting experience. Our food is so good in fact, that we are the first and only restaurant to have SIX Michelin Stars! Literally off the charts!!! How is this possible? How can such humble American food be so delicious that it goes beyond the standards of this French cuisine biased organization?!? Come visit our location to find out or check out our catering services too. Whether the food is served in house or at a catering service, the quality is just the same. Our catering utilizes a special "fresh-frozen" technique where we cook the food the same day of the catering service and freeze it precisely to maintain the freshness! I know what you're thinking, "Wait, isn't 'fresh-frozen' an oxymoron?" Why yes, yes it is... just don't think about it too much. Grub Stranding is the best!
